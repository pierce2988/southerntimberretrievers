Our Standard

Athletic. Birdy. Trainable. At home in the blind and on the couch.

Southern Timber Retrievers is being built around a simple standard: athletic Labrador Retrievers with drive, intelligence, trainability, and the kind of temperament that fits both the duck blind and the family home.

Our goal is to develop dogs through real training, hunt tests, and field work — documenting the process honestly from the very beginning.

The Process

From Puppy to Finished Retriever

01

Puppy Foundations

Crate manners, socialization, early bird introduction, and building a confident, willing prospect.

02

Obedience

Here, sit, heel, and steadiness — the control that everything in the field is built on.

03

Marks & Retrieves

Single and multiple marks, lining drills, and confident water work on land and in the flooded timber.

04

Hunt Test Preparation

Blinds, honoring, gunfire and decoy exposure — the polish that holds up under test pressure.

05

HRC Goals

Working toward Started, Seasoned, and Finished Hunter titles through HRC hunt tests.

06

SRS / Master Hunter

The long-term aim: advanced marks and blinds at the Master Hunter and SRS competition level.
